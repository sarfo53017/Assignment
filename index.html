Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,pecfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines between eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cook meats herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a blow, beat the eggs with salt and pepper untill well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the Omelette: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Add your chossen fillings before folding.
  5. Fold and serve:Fold the Omelette in half and serve hot.
